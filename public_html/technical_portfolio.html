Future project ideas

I've played hockey for almost as long as I could stand, I'd love to apply my study of computer science to my love of hockey. Currently, all of the Texas A&M Ice Hockey team's games are disorganized and hosted on an outdated and cluttered website, that not only has our own games behind a paywall but also ruins the experience of watching footage by only allowing on user at a time. In the future, I'd like to create a database that hosts and organizes our game film in a way that not only allows for a better user experience, but also lets us as a team review specific parts of our play, which would allow for even greater improvement as a team.
